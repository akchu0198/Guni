Community Nursing

Meet professionally qualified nurses to look after your needs at Ruhil Care. Our nurses are specialised in disability care and have years of experience backing their work. Our team provides a range of services, which includes domestic nursing support as well as Ruhil Home care where nurses will by your side for any medical or general assistance.

Medication management

Your caregiver and nurse will track your medical appointments, your medical history and set up an appropriate routine to improve your health.

Injury care

Our nurses will ensure that you retain your health by dressing your wound and taking care of it, as well as helping you regain strength and get back to normalcy.

Observation vital signs

The nurses conduct regular check-ups to ensure you maintain optimum health levels at all times.
