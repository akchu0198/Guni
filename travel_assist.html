Travel Assist

Ruhil Care houses supportive professionals who can boost your experiences while travelling, even if for short distances. Public transport can be a challenge to tackle, along with its unequal accessibility and discomfort for disabled individuals. We aim to make this process slightly easier and smoother so you can face lesser hassles.

Ruhil Care's Travel Assistance

Public transport can be a challenge to tackle, along with its unequal accessibility and discomfort for disabled individuals. We provide a personal assistant who will learn your interests and needs and help you decide the travel plans or schemes that suit your goals.

Personal assistant

There will be a personal assistant who will first attempt to learn your interests and needs and accordingly help you decide the travel plans or passes you may need to register for. If there are special passes or packages available for disabled persons, we can help you complete those processes.

Accessible and comfortable.

Our primary aim is to help you travel and enjoy the experience without too many hindrances stopping you from doing so. This means we will be present to assist you with any travels. If you need to make a trip outside your local area, our assistant can accompany you to any destination when booked in advance. Our assistant can also help you in navigating the new area to explore disabled- friendly locations that are accessible and comfortable.

NDIS travel assist

The procedures for applying for special permits, hailing taxis that are for the disabled, and obtaining special permissions from the local council are all services that we provide as your travel assistants. We can also inform you of the formalities to obtain an allowance from the government for travelling and general mobility.
