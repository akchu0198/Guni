Community Participation

We recognise the importance that communication and having a friendly circle have in one’s own journey. Our centre organises community-based events with activities that train and promote growth through communication, shopping, and managing social outings.

Gain Self-Esteem

Being with other people in the community and participating in outdoor and group activities can greatly improve confidence and self-reliance.

Long-Term Care

Training certain muscles and parts of the body and mind for long enough can help it stop from deteriorating since they are consistently engaged and active.

Communication

Participating in group activities can improve certain social skills – such as reading social cues and communicating using language better.

What we offer

Specialised services

In case of moderate or high-level disability, we provide the necessary equipment and other mobility services depending on the impairment so you find no discomf- ort and are able to participate freely. We will also ensure all precautions are taken to avoid the risk of accidents since outdoors are often not disability- friendly.

Social Outings to Places of Choice

It is possible to suggest outdoor visits to places of your preference along with the other members of the community. For instance, you can spend some time in the library where you can read books and get involved in book clubs or other events. You can also explore other places around the neigh- bourhood and participate in community events and activities as per your interests.

Skill Training

We regularly have sessions on developing hobbies such as painting, cooking, and gardening for which we have dedicated rooms and group sessions for further engagement and learning. These hobbies significantly impact internal skills and training such as building muscle memory, social skills, and impact general growth on a long-term basis.
