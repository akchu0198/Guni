Physiotherapy

Physiotherapy is the treatment of any physical impairments and aims to maintain normalcy for the bodily aspects of your health. It is the gradual rehabilitation and management of your physical condition to improve overall health.

At Ruhil Care, you will find the most qualified professionals to ensure you are able to return to your best self. Our physiotherapists will regularly examine you to study and understand your overall condition at first, and gradually help you engage in activities that will boost your physical strength, motor skills, posture, and overall mobility.

Ruhil Care identifies the differential needs that disabled individuals have, and our professionals are well-equipped to treat and support these difficulties to ensure that you can step out feeling better. We house experienced physiotherapists who will be personally assigned to you to understand your lifestyle, and accordingly enhance your activity schedule to gradually improve your physical functions.

Pain Management

If you suffer from chronic pain, or pain of any kind that lasts for a certain period, our physiotherapists can help by mobilising the joints, performing tissue treatments, doing therapy and stretching that can boost overall movement and reduce the pain you experience.

Stroke

After suffering from a stroke, regaining movement is essential but the pace is integral to achieving a balanced growth. Our team will begin rehabilitation as soon as medically cleared, and focus firstly on basics like getting out of bed and walking. They will plan a routine that can help you return to normalcy at your own comfortable pace while also relearning from other aspects of your life.

Incontinence

Since incontinence requires a strengthening of the pelvic floor muscles, our physiotherapists will be available to guide you into doing activities that can help in reducing experiences of incontinence by gradually strengthening the muscles that support the bladder and the urethra, and growing more independent along the way.

Chronic diseases like Diabetes, Obesity, and Osteoarthritis

Our physiotherapists understand the nature and causes of chronic diseases and will attempt to eliminate their roots by strengthening your body. For instance, our physiotherapist can recommend an exercise regimen that can reduce body fat and promote glucose processing in the case of diabetes.
