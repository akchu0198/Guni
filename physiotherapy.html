NDIS Physiotherapy

At Ruhil Care, find the most qualified professionals to ensure you are at your healthiest best. Our physiotherapists will regularly examine you and gradually help you engage in activities to boost your physical strength, motor skills, posture, and overall mobility.

Ruhil Care identifies the differential needs that disabled individuals have, and our professionals are well-equipped to treat and support these difficulties to ensure that you can step out feeling better. We house experienced physiotherapists who will be personally assigned to you to understand your lifestyle, and accordingly enhance your activity schedule to gradually improve your physical functions.

Our physiotherapy assistance includes:

  • A holistic approach towards physical improvement
  • Observation of complete health
  • Setting routines at your pace
  • Friendly and empathetic environment
  • Regular assessments

Pain Management

Our physiotherapists can relieve chronic pain by mobilising the joints, performing tissue treatments, and stretching to boost overall movement and reduce the pain you experience.

Stroke

If your loved one has suffered from a stroke, our team can help with gradual rehabilitation when medically cleared. They will plan a routine that can help you return to normalcy at your own comfortable pace while also relearning from other aspects of your life.

Neurological Conditions

Physiotherapy can treat neurological disorders that affect the spinal cord, brain and nerves. Our team can treat the mobility and physical impairments that arise from the condition.

Chronic diseases like Diabetes and Osteoarthritis

Our physiotherapists understand the nature and causes of chronic diseases. They will attempt to eliminate the roots by strengthening your body through a holistic perspective of overall growth.
