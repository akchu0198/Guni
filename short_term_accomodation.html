Short term Accomodation

Ruhil Care recognises the difficulties involved in caregiving and the differential challenges that are specific to disability care. Hence, we offer the opportunity to caregivers to take a break from their work and provide a safe, inclusive and accessible environment to disabled individuals.

Is this a government-funded program?

Short-term accommodation is covered under the National Disability Insurance Scheme and Ruhil Care accepts their funding. You can pick a plan that suits your needs and matches the funding under NDIS before taking up accommodation here and we will help you navigate the process of payment.
